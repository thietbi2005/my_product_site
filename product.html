HSTXG Series Plastic Pipe Hydrostatic Pressure Burst Tester

Application:

HSTXG Series Plastic Pipe Hydrostatic Pressure Tester is mainly used to determine the resistance of thermoplastics pipes and short-time hydraulic burst to constant internal water pressure at constant temperature. It is applicable to thermoplastic pipes intended for the conveyance of fluids.

It confirms to ISO1167,ASTM D1598 It is under the standard of ISO 1167, ASTM D1598, ISO9080, CJ/T108 , ASTM F1335 and ASTM D1599.
