HNR-400CD Touch Screen Melt Flow Indexer（MFR & MVR ）

Instrument overview

This machine is not only suitable for engineering plastics such as polyethylene, polyarylsulfone, fluoroplastics, nylon, etc., with higher melting temperature, but also suitable for plastics with lower melting temperature such as polyethylene, polystyrene, polypropylene, ABS resin, and polyoxymethylene resin. test. Especially suitable for universities, research institutes, quality inspection institutions, plastic manufacturers, plastic products and petrochemical industries.

Environmental requirements:

1) Ambient temperature: within the range of 10℃-40℃;

2) The relative humidity of the environment is within 30%-80%;
