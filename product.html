HST-MoPao5ST Metallographic Specimen Grinding and Polishing Machine

The HST-MoPao5ST automatic grinding and polishing machine is a single-disc desktop machine, which is an integrated machine with central force pressurization and single-point pressurization. It is a new generation of high-precision, automated sample preparation process grinding and polishing equipment manufactured in accordance with international standards and using international advanced technology. It has the functions of optional selection of the rotation direction of the grinding and polishing disk, quick replacement of the grinding and polishing disk, multi-sample clamp and pneumatic single-point loading. This machine adopts an advanced microprocessor control system, which makes the rotation speed of the grinding and polishing disc and grinding and polishing head infinitely adjustable, and the sample preparation pressure and time settings are intuitive and convenient. Just replace the grinding and polishing disc or sandpaper and fabric to complete the grinding and polishing process. This enables the machine to exhibit a wider range of applicability. It has the characteristics of stable rotation, safety and reliability, low noise and the use of cast aluminum base to increase the rigidity of grinding and polishing.              

- All-in-one machine for central force pressurization and single-point pressurization

- One machine with multiple functions, easily completes pre-grinding, rough grinding, fine grinding, rough polishing and fine polishing of Metallographic samples

-Double disc single control, four-level fixed speed, speed control system optimized for grinding and polishing machines

- Easy-to-control LCD screen, simple operation

- Finely ground and surface-treated work disc to ensure smooth sample surface

- The integrally formed high-strength fiberglass material shell is sturdy and durable.
