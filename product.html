HST-MLF112 EN149 mask leakage filtration efficiency testing tester

Application

: HST-MLF112 Particulate Filtration Efficiency (PFE) tester is a new generation filter performance Test Instrument. It can be widely used in the Testing of particulate filtering efficiency of air filter materials and the filtering efficiency of masks and raw materials, and can also provide resistance performance testing of test samples. Standards compliant: YY / T 0469-2011 Medical surgical mask 5.6.2 Particle filtration efficiency GB / T 32610-2016 Technical Specifications of Daily Protective Masks Appendix A Filtration Efficiency Test Method GB 2626-2019 Respiratory protection Self-priming filter anti-particulate respirator 6.3 Filtration efficiency GB 19082-2009 Technical requirements for medical disposable protective clothing 5.7 Filtration efficiency EN 149-2001; EN 143; JIS T8151; NIOSH 42 CFR Part 84 etc.
