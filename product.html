Device for determining the strength of the fastening of the welt and insole (up to 3 kN)

Application

: The device is designed to determine the strength of the fastening of the welt and the insole of leather shoes of nailed, screw, wooden-studded, pierced fastening of soles at a temperature of (20 +15 -10 ) Â° C according to GOST 9134-78. The maximum width of the fastened end is 50 mm, the maximum thickness of the fastened end is 35 mm. The fixture is attached to the standard grips of the Testing machine in the tensile zone using rectangular adapters. The device is allowed to be used with a load of up to 3 kN.
