Electronic Rubber Tensile Extensometer

Electronic Rubber Tensile Extensometer

The long travel extensometer, large deformation extensometer is specially developed for the Testing of materials with large elongation (such as plastic, rubber, silicone, polyurethane, etc.).

According to ASTMD412 and ISO37 standards, it is very suitable for testing dumbbell-shaped elastic specimens. This extensometer always be used together with the electronic universal testing machine. Specifications:

Range 25mm to 800mm
Range resolution 0.001mm
Accuracy 0.01 mm %
Max.sample thickness 10 mm
Max.sample width 30 mm
