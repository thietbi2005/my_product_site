ISO22609 ASTM F1862 Medical Face Mask Synthetic Blood Penetration Tester

Application

: The Masks Synthetic Blood Penetration tester is used to determine the resistance of medical protective masks to penetration by synthetic blood at different Test pressures,and can also be used to determine the resistance to blood penetration of other coating materials. Standards compliant: GB 19083-2010 Technical requirements of medical face masks - Anti-penetration property of 5.5 synthetic blood. YY/T 0691-2008 Clothing for protection against infectious agents-Medical face masks - Test method for resistance against penetration by synthetic blood (fixed volume, horizontally projected)   YY 0469-2011 Technical requirements of medical face masks– Blood penetration test apparatus. ISO 22609:2004 Clothing for protection against infectious agents - Medical face masks - Test method for resistance against penetration by synthetic blood (fixed volume, horizontally projected)   ASTM F1862-07 Standard Test Method for Resistance of Medical Face Masks to Penetration by Synthetic Blood (Horizontal Projection of Fixed Volume at a Know Velocity)
