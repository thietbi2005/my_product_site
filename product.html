Optical Fiber Cable Flexing Testing Machine GQR-1000 Series

1 Overview

  GNZ-1000 Series Optical Fiber Cable Flexing Testing Machine is intended to establish the ability of a fiber optic cable to withstand mechanical repeated flexing in service, e.g. elevator cable.
