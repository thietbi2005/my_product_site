Servo linear actuator

Brief introduction of servo linear actuator HNC series: HNC series servo linear actuators are the laTest generation of servo linear actuators introduced by our group. Three-dimensional gap-free joint supports are installed at both ends to eliminate the damage of the actuator from the lateral force from the sample to the maximum. The seat adopts unitized, standardized and modular design, and can be flanged after being removed. It has the characteristics of high response, low damping and long life. The starting pressure is less than 0.05Mpa. Main technical specifications of servo linear actuator NCA series:
