Optical Fiber Cable Repeated Bending Testing Machine GWQ-1000 Series

1 Overview

GWQ-1000 Series Optical Fiber Cable Repeated bending testing machine is intended to establish the ability of a fiber optic cable to withstand repeated bending (cyclic flexing).

2 Technical Parameters

Bending angle

±90° degree

Max. bending cycles

1 - 9999

Frequency range

10 - 30 cycles/min

Bending radius (mm)

25, 50, 75, 100, 150, 500 (others upon request)

Mass of weights for applying tension

Weight structure 27.5kg, 3 pieces of 5kg weight increment, 1 piece of 2.5kg weight increment.

Dimensions (L*W*H)

Working frame

3,000*720*2,500 mm

Control console

400*300 * 800 mm

Power
