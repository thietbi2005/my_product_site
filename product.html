HST-934.1s Barcol hardness tester

Application

: The Barcol Hardness Tester is an indentation type Hardness Tester. It is fast, simple, and easy to Test. It can be used to quickly test the hardness of aluminum and aluminum alloy materials and meet the American standard ASTM B648. Technical Parameters: Model:HST-934-1s Range:0~100HBa Effective measurement range:equivalent to 25~135HBW Indication error:42~52 HBa ±2.0 HBa;84~88 HBa ±1.0 HBa
