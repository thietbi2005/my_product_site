HST-9341 Barcol Hardness Tester

1. Overview: The Barcol Hardness Tester is an indentation type Hardness Tester. It is fast, simple, and easy to Test. It can be used to quickly test the hardness of aluminum and aluminum alloy materials and meet the American standard ASTM B648.

2.Technical Parameters

Range

0～100HBa

Effective measurement range

equivalent to 25～135HBW

Resolution

0.5HBa

Indication error

42～52 HBa ±2.0 HBa

84～88 HBa ±1.0 HBa

Repeatability error

42-52 HBa ±2.5 HBa

84～88 HBa ±1.5 HBa
