TNS-A Atomatic Spring torsion testing machine(5n.m-50n.m)

Application:

This series is a new type of automatic control vertical spring torsion testing machine developed by our company. It is mainly suitable for Testing the torsion angle and torsion torque of torsion springs, scroll springs, elastic components and friction mechanisms. It can measure not only the torque corresponding to a certain twist angle, but also the twist angle corresponding to a certain torque.

This machine complies with "JB/T 9370-1999", "JJG 269-2006" Torsion Testing Machine standards and spring manufacturing Test standards. It can conduct tests and provide data according to national standards and international standards such as ISO, JIS, ASTM, DIN, etc. It is an ideal testing equipment for spring manufacturing, low-voltage electrical appliances, power machinery, quality inspection departments, colleges and universities, scientific research laboratories and other departments.
