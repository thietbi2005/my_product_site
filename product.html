HST-BD07 Whiteness Tester

Application:

It is a special instrument for measuring the whiteness of objects. Widely used in whiteness measurement of paper and cardboard, textile printing and dyeing, paints and coatings, chemical building materials, plastic products, cement, calcium carbonate powder, ceramics, enamel, porcelain clay, talc, starch, flour, salt, detergents, cosmetics and other objects.
