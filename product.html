Optical Fiber Cable Impact Testing Machine GCJ-1000 Series

Overview

GCJ-1000 Series Optical Fiber Cable Impact Testing Machine is intended to determine the ability of optical fiber cable withstand impact

IEC-60794-1-2-E4  

2 Technical Parameters  

2.1. Impacting height: 300mm, 1000mm, adjustable.

2.2. Mass of hammer

2.3. Six combinations of 0.5Kg, 1Kg, 2Kg, 3Kg, and 6Kg.

2.4. testing times range: 1-9999.

2.5. Setting range of frequency

Setting range of Testing times for national standard: 10～20 times/min. Setting range of testing times for military standard: 10～30 times/min.

2.6. Power supply: 3-phase 4-wire, 50Hz, 2KW.

2.7. Ambient temperature: 10℃～40℃.

2.8. Ambient humidity: less than 80% (Non-condensing).
