HST-6300B 300j Plastic Pipe Falling Weight Impact Tester

It is suitable for the determination of impact toughness of PVC-U, PVC-C pipe, PP pipe, PE pipe, plastic pipe for underground communication pipe, etc. It is an ideal Test instrument for Testing, production units, building materials industry and scientific research units. The control part of the Test machine adopts PLC control, touch screen operation display, high degree of automation, digital preset and display impact height, lift hammer and drop hammer; anti-secondary impact adopts pneumatic transmission device, and the secondary impact capture rate is 100%. With personal safety automatic protection device (sample room door safety protection, anti-shock safety protection, etc.). The instrument is simple, safe, reliable and beautiful in appearance, and is exported to Europe and the United States and other countries.
