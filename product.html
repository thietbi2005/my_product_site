HST-PNZ electro-hydraulic servo torsional fatigue test machine

Application:

HST-FN electro-hydraulic servo torsional fatigue Test bench is suitable for torsional fatigue Testing of products such as  cardan shaft  and constant shafts. As  required, relevant fatigue life tests may be carried out on other types of structural members and assemblies according to relevant standards.
