HST-L800 Integrated floor thickness gauge

Application

: HST-L800 Integrated floor thickness gauge is a portable and non-destructive Testing instrument for measuring the thickness of concrete or other non-ferromagnetic media. When used, the transmitter and receiver probes are placed on the two relative testing surfaces of the concrete, respectively, to transmit and receive electromagnetic signals, and the instrument is connected according to the connection. The received signal strength is used to measure the concrete thickness.
