Device for testing the bending of the toe of shoes (up to 3 kN)

Application:

The device is designed to Test the flexibility of the toe of shoes along the bend line at an angle of 25 ° at a temperature of (20 +15 -10 ) ° C in accordance with GOST 9718-88. The maximum width of the toe of the half-pair is 100 mm. The maximum height of the half-pair toe is 35 mm. The minimum distance from the fold to the edge of the toe is 90 mm. The fixture is attached to the standard universal adapter of the Testing machine in the stretch zone. The device is allowed to be used with a load of up to 3 kN.
