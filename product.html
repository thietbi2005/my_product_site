RTTS Video Extensometer

RTTS Video Extensometer is an optical non-contact type three-dimentional deformation measuring system based on DIC(Digital Image Correlation)measurement method. It can accurately measure the 2D & 3D strain property of materials and components. Meanwhile, it can also be applied for Testing and displacement measurement of 3D Profile-Displacement-Deformation of various metals and nonmetals, under static & dynamic deformation Tests.

Application

s:

Tensile test for bar specimen at 1000℃ Tensile test for smaller metal sheet with 12mm gauge length Tensile test for film specimen Tensile test for plastic specimen with dual camera system（Stitched Scene） Poisson’s ratio measurement for carbon fibre composite with dual camera system Three point bending test for wooden specimen
