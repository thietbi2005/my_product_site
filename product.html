Medical surgical mask flame retardant performance tester

The flame retardant performance tester for medical surgical masks is used for the flame retardant performance Tester of surgical masks. Meet the Test standard YY0469-2011 medical surgical masks. Features 1. The flame temperature can be adjusted. 2. Digital display of flame temperature. 3. Automatically move to detect. Detector technical parameters 1. Flame temperature 800 ± 50 ℃. 2. The movement speed of the head mold is 60 ± 5mm / s. 3. Flame height 40 ± 4mm. 4. The distance between the burning top and the bottom of the sample is 20 ± 2mm
