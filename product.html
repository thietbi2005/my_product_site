TIME7212 Portable Vibration Meter

This instrument is suitable for conventional vibration measurement, especially vibration measurement in rotating or reciprocating machinery. It can not only measure vibration acceleration, velocity, and displacement, but also record and print out.

Convenient and quick key combination calibration mode;

Can store 100 sets of measurement values;’

While ensuring measurement sensitivity, it also improves measurement stability;

Used for rapid inspection of motors, fans, pumps, compressors, machine tools and other equipment and preventive inspections for failures of rotating machinery and equipment;

With over-limit automatic alarm indication function;

With undervoltage indication function;

With date and time setting function;

Date and measured value can be printed for archiving and recording;

Can store 100 measured values
