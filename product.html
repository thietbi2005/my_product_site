HST-RD DIN53516 ISO4649 Rubber DIN Abrasion Tester

Application:

It's to determine the abrasion resistance elastic material, rubber, tires, convey belt, transmit belt, sole, synthetic leather, leather and others.The abrasive resistance is assessed by measuring the abrasion loss after the specimen is rubbed against sandpaper.

Standards:

DIN  ISO 4649:2006-11 (E). Rubber, vulcanized or thermoplastic - Determination of abrasion resistance using a rotating cylindrical drum device.

ASTM  D5963 - 04(2010)e1 Standard Test Method for Rubber Property—Abrasion Resistance (Rotary Drum Abrader).
