Roller clamp for testing tapes (up to 5 kN)

Application

: The gripper is intended for Testing tapes for adhesion at an angle of 90 ° at a temperature of (20 +15 -10 ) °С of samples made of rubber, soft polymers, adhesives, sealants up to 16 mm thick and up to 60 mm wide. The fixture is attached to the standard universal adapter of the Testing machine in the stretch zone. The device is allowed to be used with a load of up to 5 kN.
