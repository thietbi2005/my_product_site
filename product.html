JBS-C 300j,450j,750j Touch screen Digital Display Charpy impact testing machine

Application

:

JBS-C Touch screen Digital Display Semi-automatic Charpy Impact Testing Machine is used for measuring the metal material resistance performance under dynamic load, in order to determine the material properties under dynamic load.

It’s a necessary testing machine, not only used in the metallurgy, machine manufacture etc areas, but also used for the science research.
