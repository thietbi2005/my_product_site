HST-HBRV250STE Universal Hardness Tester with Touch Screen

Model HST-HBRV250STE is a multifunctional Brinell, Rockwell and Vickers integrated hardness Testing machine. To cover a variety of Hardness Testing methods, a wide Test force range is designed: from 3 kg to 250 kg to perform hardness Testing on many different materials and components in various industries.

Specifically designed to provide industry-leading user-friendliness and rugged construction, the machine delivers clear images for accurate and repeatable Vickers, Rockwell and Brinell Hardness Test measurements in the laboratory as well as in original production environments. It is ideal for educational institutions such as technical schools, universities and multifunctional general workshops.

The machine comes standard with an 8-inch LCD touch screen, CCD camera (optional), automatic measurement software (optional) and consumables for all 3 testing methods, providing a turnkey solution for your hardness testing needs.
