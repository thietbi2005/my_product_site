Dumbbell Specimen Making Machine

Application

： The dumbbell-shaped sample preparation machine adopts mechanical processing means to prepare various non-metallic materials for the special equipment of the standard sub-bell sample for tensile test. The samples prepared by this machine meet the various dimensions and technical requirements of GB1040-92 samples. Technical parameter:
Milling cutter diameter Φ30mm
Cutter speed 5800 rpm
Working table size (length × width) 400×240mm
Sample thickness 1~30mm
Weight 50kg
Power supply voltage AC 220V
Power 550W
