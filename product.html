Fabric Bursting Strength Tester HS-T07

Application:

Fabric Bursting Strength tester is used to determination of pressure for a variety of fabrics, including knits, woven fabric, non-woven fabric, laminated fabric and other craft-made variety of fabrics, in standard conditions or under wet bursting strength and bursting dilation.This bursting strength Testing machine is also called mullen bursting Tester.
