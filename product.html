HST-T Series is the 3D manual type Video Measuring System developed by HST Group

It is designed for 3D measurement like slope, round, bad ditch, columns, sphere, dimensions, blind hole, and other geometrical terms like distance between the elements, angle on the workpiece.

1. It can measure point, line, circle, arc and oval, rectangular and can improve the accuracy with the multi-point positioning function.

2. Programmable integrated 8 regional light control, achieve professional surface measurement.

3. ‘Image+Probe’ measuring method, equipped with Renishaw™ probe and our company's self-developed 3D metrology software, can achieve rapid measurement with high accuracy.   4. With UWC series professional metrology controller, each probe touch can be heard with beep, more accurate probe operation. 5. Grade 00 JINANQING granite base and column, high stability.
