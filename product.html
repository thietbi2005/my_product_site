HST-MoPao2T Grinding And Polishing Machine

Application

: HST-MoPao2T is a newly designed touch screen type Metallographic specimen grinding and polishing machine which is equipped with double discs, conversion functions between Chinese and English, and timing function. It can realize the whole process of rough grinding, fine grinding, rough polishing and finishing polishing for specimen preparing. This machine is the ideal specimen preparing instrument in factories, scientific research institutes and laboratories of colleges. Technical specifications: Power supply: 380V, 50Hz Diameter of working disc: 203 mm Rotating speed of working disc: 150 /300 / 600 / 800 rpm (four-level constant speed) Motor power: YSS7124, 550W Dimensions: 860 x 660 x 355 mm Weight: 47kg
