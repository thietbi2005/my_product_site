XJU Series 2.75~22J Dial display Plastic Izod Impact Testing Machine

1. Product introduction:

It is mainly used to measure the impact toughness of non-metallic materials such as hard plastics, reinforced nylon, glass fiber reinforced plastics, ceramics, cast stone, and electrical insulating materials. Ideal Testing equipment for chemical industry, scientific research units, colleges and universities, quality inspection departments, professional manufacturer laboratories and other units.

2. Executive standard:

- ISO179-2000 "Plastic-Hard Material-Determination of Impact Strength of Simply Supported Beams" - GB/T1043-2008 "Impact Test method of rigid plastic simply supported beam" - JB/T8762-1998 "Plastic Simply Supported Beam Impact Testing Machine" - GB/T18743-2002 "Thermoplastic pipe simply supported beam impact test method for fluid transportation" (applicable to pipe fittings)
