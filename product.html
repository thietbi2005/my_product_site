HST-MR Rubber Rheometer Machine

Rotorless Rheometer is used to determine the vulcanization characteristics of a rubber compound. It can dynamically display temperature curve and curve of shear modulus- vulcanization time under set temperature and pressure. It consists of main machine, temperature determination and control system, sensor, computer, data collector, printer and electrical components. It completely conforms to ASTM D5289, ISO 6502, GB/T 16584, etc.
