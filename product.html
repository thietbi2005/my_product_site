HST JBI-300B Metal IZOD Impact Tester

HST JBI-300B Metal IZOD Impact Testing Machine

Application:

IZOD Impact Testing Machine is used to Test anti-impact performance of metal material under dynamic load to judge the nature of material under dynamic load.  This machine is semi-automatic Testing machine, easy to operate and high efficiency. They can make use of spare energy to raise pendulum automatically and be ready for next impact test, can carry out the impact test continuously. It is widely used in the laboratory, metallurgy, machinery manufacturing industry, etc. Standards: ISO R84 , B·S 131 part 4, GB4158-84
