HVS-1000ZT Touch Screen Automatic Turret Digital Display Micro Vickers Hardness Tester

I. Scope of Applocation： HVS-1000ZT Touch screen automatic turret digital display microVickers Hardness Tester It is mainly used for the measurement of the micro Vickers hardness of various metals and some non-metal materials. It can Test various parts (machined parts, forgings, cast iron, steel castings, etc.), non-ferrous metals, thermal after-treatment parts, and various micro And thin parts, glass, ceramics, agate, gemstones, etc. for hardness measurement, at the same time suitable for carbonization, quenching and hardening layer effective depth, coating, surface coating and welding parts heat-affected part measurement. It can also be used to observe the internal Metallographic structure of various metal parts and to collect, display and output the image. It is especially suitable for measuring the hardness gradient distribution curve of the workpiece and the surface hardness of the gear after heat treatment, and the effective hardening layer depth.
