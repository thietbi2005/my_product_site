HST-304H Oxygen Index Test Apparatus

Application:

It is used to judge the difficulty of burning when the material is in contact with flame in the air. It is suitable for homogeneous solid materials, laminated materials, soft sheets and film materials.

Standards:

Comply with GB/T2406 "Plastic Flammability Test Method-Oxygen Index Method" and JIS7201, BS2782, ANSI/ASTM, D2863, ISO4589, test standards.
