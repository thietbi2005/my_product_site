HST NSM525-E Electric Dumbbell Specimen Making Machine

Application

: This dumbbell type sample making machine is a special equipment mainly used for making standard sample strips of non-metallic materials. This equipment has the characteristics of simple operation, fast sample making, standard sample and stable size; it is suitable for pipe production enterprises, product quality supervision and inspection institutes, building science research institutes, etc. to make standard sample strips for functional and performance Testing of non-metallic materials such as plastics. 2.
