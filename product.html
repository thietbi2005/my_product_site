HST-BG30 Belt Surface Grinding Machine

Application

And Features: HST-BG30 Belt Surface Grinding Machine is a new generation design, made according to the international standard and advanced process technique. It only takes 1~2 minutes to for each belt to finish the operation. For the grinding of spectroscopy specimen, it is very economic to save time, labour and abrasive paper.
