Electro-hydraulic Steel Bar Tensile Testing machine(2000KN,3000KN)

Application

WAW-Q Series Steel Bar Tensile Testing Machine is designed with single workspace. It can do tension, compression, bending and shearing Tests. Force measurement is through load cell. With long travel actuator stroke, it is suitable to test standard specimens, long length specimens, and specimens with large elongation.
