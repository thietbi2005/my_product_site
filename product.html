HST-TLW Computer Automatic Spring Compression Load Testing Machine（0.5-100KN)

This Testing machine is mainly used to measure the tensile Test and compression test of various coil springs. It can test both the spring load under a certain deformation and the spring deformation under a certain load. Mainly used in spring manufacturers, low-voltage electrical appliances, machinery, universities and research laboratories.
