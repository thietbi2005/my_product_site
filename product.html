HSCTT Series Electronic High-temperature Endurance Creep Testing Machine

Application

Testing machine is mainly used to measure performance and creep rupture strength properties of various metals and alloys at high temperatures, it is special equipment for studying and testing materials performance in the iron and steel metallurgy, aerospace, scientific research institutes, universities, and quality inspection departments. 2.
