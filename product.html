A14.404-AW Computerized Research Grade Metallurgical Microscope

The A14.404-AW computerized research-grade Metallographic microscope is a trinocular inverted metallographic Microscope with a newly designed infinity optical system. It can be widely used in casting, smelting, heat treatment research, raw material inspection or material processing analysis Inverted microscope. The machine comprehensively covers various observation methods such as bright field, dark field, simple polarized light, differential interference, etc., and adapts to various research needs.
