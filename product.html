Horizontal Profile Projector HST CPJ-3020W

Product name：Horizontal Profile Projector HST CPJ-3020W Product categories：Profile Projector

Horizontal profile projector HST CPJ-3020W is a photoelectric measuring system of high precision and efficiency can inspect all kinds of surface and outline of the complicated work pieces such as cam, template, tread, gear, perform willing cutter and so on. It requires in the checking department and workshop and is widely used in machinery, mould, tool, electricity and light industry. ▶   300mm diameter screen with digital protractor. ▶   200X100mm precision movement work-stage. ▶   Work-stage sway range between  15°. ▶   Halogen profile and fiber surface illumination adjustable. ▶   Geometric multifunction data processing system upgrade to DRO DC-3000. ▶   Built-in Mini-printer. ▶   Available 10x,20x,50x,100x objective, over-chart and foot-switch edge detector etc.
