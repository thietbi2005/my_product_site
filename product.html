MIKROSIZE MS995 High - frequency Infrared Carbon and Sulfur Analyzer

The MS995 Infrared Carbon and Sulfur Analyzer, used with the WF - 88 High - frequency Induction Furnace, can quickly and accurately measure the mass fraction of carbon and sulfur in materials like steel, iron, alloys, foundry core sand, non - ferrous metals, cement, chemicals, ores, coke, catalysts, etc. It's a high - tech product integrating optics, mechanics, electronics, computer science, and analytical techniques. It incorporates advanced foreign tech, featuring a wide measurement range, strong anti - interference, comprehensive functions, easy operation, and reliable results.
