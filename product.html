HST-QTJ200 Film Strip Cutting Machine

HST-QTJ200 Film Strip Cutting Machine is a special equipment for preparing samples for film tensile Testing. It can cut PET polyester film with a thickness of less than 0.5mm or other materials that can be cut with a blade.

The machine adopts spring latch mechanical control and is equipped with a special blade. It can quickly complete the preparation of tensile specimens, greatly improve the efficiency of tensile testing, avoid the errors caused by traditional manual cutting methods, effectively reduce the errors of Test results, and ensure product quality performance. It is one of the necessary equipment for various film tensile tests.
