HST-TN Vertical Spring Torsion Testing Machine(500-10000N.mm)

1.

The vertical automatic digital display touch screen spring torsion testing machine mainly does torsion Test on various torsion springs and spiral coil springs. Measuring the torque of the spring under a certain Angle, or measuring the Angle of the spring under a certain torque, torsion spring stiffness test, can also be used for other elastic components and friction mechanism torsion performance test, the spring testing machine is mainly used in spring manufacturers, electrical manufacturers, power machinery factories, colleges and universities and related research units. It can automatically complete the whole cycle test environment of the spring, and can also determine a certain parameter of the spring separately. Users can adjust the test program according to actual needs in use, and the operation is very convenient.
