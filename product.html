HSTBF-102H High Temperature Rotary Bending Fatigue Testing Machine

1.Function Rotary Bending fatigue testing machine, with the following functions: 1)Meet the requirements of four-point tensile Test of cylindrical specimen of GB/T 4337-2015, and carry out the test; 2)Can be tested at high temperature or normal temperature; 3)Servo motor drive, speed stepless adjustment; 4)Counting function, accurately record the number of sample rotation; 5)Servo motor loading, force sensor to measure the loading force (moment); 6)The test speed can be changed during operation; 7)The loading force (bending moment) can be changed during operation; 8)Automatically shut down after sample failure and save data; 9)Calculate and process data, generate and print test reports. 2.Applicable standards: (1)GB/T 4337-2015 "Rotating Bending Method for Fatigue test of Metal Materials" (2)GB/T 2611-2007 "General Technical Requirements for Testing machines" (3) HB5152-1996 "Metal Room Temperature Rotational Bending Fatigue Test Method"

(4) HB5153-1996 "Metal High Temperature Rotational Bending Fatigue Test Method"
