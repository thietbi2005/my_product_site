XJUD 2.75,5.5,11,22J Touch Screen Display Izod Impact Testing Machine

Instrument overview

It is mainly used to measure the impact toughness of non-metallic materials such as hard plastics, reinforced nylon, glass fiber reinforced plastics, ceramics, cast stone, and electrical insulating materials. Ideal Testing equipment for chemical industry, scientific research units, colleges and universities, quality inspection departments, professional manufacturer laboratories and other units. Features It is a simple structure, convenient operation, accurate and reliable data. Original Taiwan Delta touch screen display, PLC control, With a micro printer With USB connect to the computer directly. measuring instrument, which accurately transmits the shape of the sample to the host through the notch depth measuring instrument, and saves the data to improve the accuracy of the experimental results.
