HST-1208 Ink Friction Decolorization Tester

This machine is used to Test the adhesion of ink to judge the quality of ink. This machine can be used for dry grinding test, wet grinding test, discoloration change test, paper blur test and special friction test. The ink abrasion test is a test method designed to evaluate the abrasion or abrasion resistance of ink on paper or cardboard.
