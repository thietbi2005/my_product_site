Roller rolling for testing tapes for adhesion

Application

: The rolling roller is designed for gluing strips to the base when preparing samples for Testing tapes for adhesion at an angle of 90 ° at a temperature of (20 +15 -10 ) ° C for samples made of rubber, soft polymers, adhesives, sealants up to 60 mm wide. The mass of the roller is strictly regulated 2.7 ± 0.05 kg.
