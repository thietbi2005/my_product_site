Three point bend test fixture (up to 1000 kN)

Application

: Device for Testing three-point bending, used for testing static bending of samples of metals and building materials with a width of up to 140 mm, has a support radius of 15 mm, a loading tip radius of 15 mm and allows you to spread the supports to a distance of up to 340 mm. The maximum load of application of the device is up to 1000 kN.
