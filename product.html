HSTWDT 35 Microcomputer controlled electronic ladder static load testing machine

HSTWDT 35 microcomputer control electronic step static Test bench adopts servo motor automatic control technology, which is suitable for static test loading of escalators and moving walkway steps, step kick boards and pedals. Standard: GB/T16899-2011 "Safety regulations for the manufacture and installation of escalators and moving walkways" TSGT7024-2004 "Escalator and moving walkway pedal type test rules" Main technical parameters and technical indicators:
