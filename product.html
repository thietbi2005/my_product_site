Device for determining the fastening strength of medium and high heels (up to 3 kN)

Application

: The device is designed to determine the fastening strength of medium and high heels at a temperature of (20 +15 -10 ) ° C according to GOST 9136-72. Maximum grip opening 58 mm, maximum heel height 44 mm. Tilt angle ratings -30°, -15°, 0°, +15°, +30° relative to the tension axis. The height of the vise is 55 mm. The fixture is attached to standard universal adapters of the Testing machine in the tension zone. The device is allowed to be used with a load of up to 3 kN.
