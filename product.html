HST- JZ500 Manual Type High Strength Bolt Detector (with manual wrench)

Digital display manual type high-strength bolt detector, the detector is designed and manufactured according to GB50205-2001 "Steel structure engineering Acceptance Code". It can Test, display and print the axial force, torque and torque coefficient of large hexagon head high-strength bolt connection pair (M16,M20,M22,M24,M27,M30) andtorsional shear high-strength bolt connection pair(M16,M20,M22,M24,M27,M30).
