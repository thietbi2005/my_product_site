HST DT Series Drive Shaft Torsion Fatigue Testing Machine

Application

: This tesing machine is used for torsional fatigue and static strength Tests of drive shafts. The tests are carried out under variable loads and adjustable test setups, in order to achieve a required number of cycles. Switch-off criteria are either the attainment of defined torsion angle or the breaking of the test specimen.
