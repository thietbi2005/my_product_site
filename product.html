HST-PM-500/1000kN Servo hydraulic pulsation fatigue testing machine

The machine is mainly suitable for static compression molding Test and dynamic one-way pulsation fatigue test of various large-scale components such as bridges, trusses, automobile chassis, front and rear axles, locomotive frame, chassis and various concrete structural parts. The machine has the remarkable features of reliable design, simple operation, energy saving and low operating cost.

Various specifications of actuators ( 50KN 100KN 250KN 500KN 1000KN ) are fully factory-tested or supplied separately to the user for complete Testing of multi-station (multi-spot). They can also be used with any host. Use to keep the value accurate and stable.
