HST-F Series is the enhanced type manual Video Measuring System developed by HST Group.

It has been widely used in machinery, electronics, metalwork, mould, plastic and other industries for precision measurement.

1. It adopts TEO™ 1/2'' colors CCD camera, and professional optical zoom lens and the cross-line generator as a measure aiming system.

2. Digital measurement and data processing system consists of two coordinate’s studio, optical linear scale.

3. It has a variety of data processing, display, input, output, in particular, is very useful function of the work piece straighten.

4. It has R232 interface with a computer and specialized measurement software which can also be processed and output circular mapping.

5. Grade 00 JINANQING granite base and column, high stability.
