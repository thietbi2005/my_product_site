HST-RA ASTM D1630 Rubber NBS Abrasion Tester

Application

Determine the resistance to abrasion of vulcanized rubber or other compounds, or both, used for the soles and heels of footwear. It is not recommended for materials less than 2.54 mm (0.1 in.) in thickness.

Principle Place Test piece on the Test machine, add a certain load in order to rotate rubbing specimen of sandpaper, and check its abradability.

Feature 1. Equip with 3 dials and can set prewear thickness to test. 2. LCD controlled, automatically stop when test finished 3. Equip with vacuum cleaner and clear wear powder timely.
