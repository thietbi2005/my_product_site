ISO 7263 Lab CMT CCT Corrugated Paper Croove Test Machine

Application:

Computer fluter, (also known as corrugated base paper corrugator) is the basic Test for corrugated base paper after corrugated in the laboratory (called corrugated paper), to conduct the flat crush strength (CMT) and compressive strength (CCT) test The equipment needs to be used together with the ring pressure sampler and ring pressure strength tester.

The parameters and functions of this instrument comply with the following national Standards:

GB / T 1061 "Grooving Instrument"

ISO 7263 "Corrugating medium-Determination of the flat crush resistance after laboratory fluting"
