HSTCT Plastic Piping Thermal Cycling Tester

Application

Applicable to the Testing of various plastic pipes and pipe fittings assembled in accordance with the requirements of the standard. The pipe system bears a certain internal pressure and alternates between cold and hot temperatures within a specified time. After the specified number of Tests, check the pipes and fittings. Leakage or rupture. Standard: ISO 10508, ISO 15874, ISO 15875,ISO 15876,ISO 15877,EN 12293, ASTM F 1335-98 and the equivalent.
