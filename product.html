P-2 metallographic sample polishing machine（combined type）

Application

: Model P-2 metallographic specimen polishing machine is suitable for the polishing of the specimen which has been grinded. The specimen surface is very smooth after processing and can be used to observe and measure the Metallographic structure of specimen under microscope.
