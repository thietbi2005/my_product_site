HST-100 Series Plastic Moisture Meter

Product introduction

This series rapid moisture analyzer is equipped with a high-efficiency halogen lamp heating device on the basis of a precision electromagnetic force weighing sensor to achieve rapid and accurate moisture determination. This durable and affordable moisture analyzer benefits from rich experience in precision instrument development, design, and manufacturing, as well as years of moisture measurement expertise. Suitable for research institutes, universities, inspection institutes, metrology institutes, enterprises and institutions, widely used in various industries such as plastic, grain, feed, powder, tobacco, paper, food (dehydrated vegetables, meat, noodles, flour, biscuits, pies) , Aquatic processing), tea, beverages, grains, chemical raw materials, pharmaceuticals, textile raw materials, etc., Test the free ion water contained in the samples.
