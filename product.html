Device "Snail" for tensile testing of wire up to 10 mm (up to 100 kN)

Application

: Device "Snail" for Testing for static tension at a temperature of (20 +15 -10 ) ° С wire made of metals and their alloys with a diameter or maximum cross-sectional size not exceeding 10 mm of round section or other section up to 80 mm 2 according to GOST 10446- 80. The fixture is attached to the standard universal adapter of the Testing machine in the stretch zone. The device is allowed to be used with a load of up to 100 kN.
