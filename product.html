Thermal shock Test chamber

Product Description The rubber thermal shock climatic chamber,  indispensable in materials industry such as metal, plastic, rubber, electronics etc, is applicable to test temperature endurance of material structure and composite materials under a rapid shift from extremely high temperature to a extremely low temperature or reverse, so as to find in shortest time the chemical changes or physical damages caused by thermal expansion and contraction.
