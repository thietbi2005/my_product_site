HST-7000 Metallographic Microscope

Application

HST-7000 Series Metallographic Microscope used for microscopic observation of opaque objects. This type has Epi-illuminator, long achromatic objective lens (no cover glass), wide-field eyepieces and set polarizer device. Image clarity, easy operate.  It is the ideal instrument for metal, mineralogy, precision engineering research. Suit for schools, research, factories and other departments. Specification
