HST-UL94 Horizontal Flammability Tester and Vertical Burning Tester

Equipment description:

This Horizontal and vertical flammability tester is applicable to solid materials and the apparent density measured according to  GB6343 not less than 250kg/m2 of foam when placed in the horizontal and vertical with a small flame ignition source ignited  combustion after the Test. The machine has the use of safe, reliable, easy to operate, and so on. Meet the standard: This Horizontal and vertical flammability testr according to national standards GB/T2408-1996 "Test method for flammability of  plastic - horizontal and vertical Law," flame-retardant material designed to test equipment.
