HST5040 Electronic Sample Notching Machine

Introduction:

The electric notch sample preparation machine is a cantilever beam, simply supported beam type impact tester for non-metallic material impact toughness Test notched sample preparation equipment, this equipment can be used for scientific research institutions, universities, non-metallic material manufacturers and other units The laboratory made notched samples for use.

This machine meets the requirements of ISO179-2000, ISO180-2000 and GB/T1043-2008, GB/T1843-2008 standards. It is a simple structure, fast and accurate operation method, can mill one or more samples at a time, and has accuracy Very high sample preparation equipment.
