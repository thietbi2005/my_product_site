BTP- 60kN Erichsen Cupping Testing Machine

This machine adopts mechatronics design, neat and elegant appearance. It is a precision equipment for performing process performance Tests on metal sheets and strips. It can realize GB/T4156-2020 / ISO 20482-2013 "Metal Material Sheets and Strips Eric The Mori Cupping Test Method" meets the requirements of JJG 583-88 and JB/T7408-94, and also adopts the standard of ASTM E643-2008 to test the plastic deformation performance of metal sheets and strips during the test.
