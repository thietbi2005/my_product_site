50kN,100kN Robot automatic electromechanical tensile testing machine

Robot automatic electronic tensile Testing machine is composed of electronic tensile host, equipped with automatic Extensometer, code scanning system, weighing and length measuring device, ABB robot, sample holder, control system, software, etc. The fixture of the testing machine is hydraulic Flat push clamps.

Robot automatic electronic tensile Testing machine is used for tensile testing of batches of bar samples, and is suitable for automatic measurement of tensile performance indicators of hot-rolled ribbed steel bars. It is an ideal testing equipment for industrial and mining enterprises, scientific research institutes, colleges and universities, commodity inspection arbitration, technical supervision and other departments to conduct tensile tests on batches of bar samples.
