HST-135 Plastic Film Falling Dart Impact Tester

Application:

Electric Falling Dart Impact tester is used to determine the impact resistance by free-falling dart method. It can be used for plastic films, sheets and composite films e.g. PE preservative films, wrapping films, PET sheets and other food package and heavy package, aluminum foils and aluminum plastic composite films, paper and paper board etc. It can meet the request both of method A and method B. Standard: ISO 7765, ASTM D1709, JIS K7124
