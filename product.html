HST-ZXQ6P Automatic Metallographic Specimen Pneumatic Mounting Press

HST-ZXQ6P automatic mold-taking Metallographic and petrographic sample mounting machine is a process before sample mounting and grinding and polishing. It can mount metallographic and petrographic samples that are small and difficult to hold or irregular. After mounting, it is convenient to grind and polish the sample and to observe the structure of the material normally under a metallographic microscope.

This machine can automatically heat, pressurize, keep warm, and cool. After pressing and forming, the pressure will be automatically released and the sample will be taken out automatically. Ready for pickup. It is a kind that only requires manual feeding, and automatically completes inlaying and mold taking. A device that reduces the tedious manual operation of taking molds. Note: Only for thermosetting materials (such as bakelite powder, etc.), the temperature has been adjusted and automatically controlled.
