Multi-station Electronic Universal Testing Machine

Microcomputer controlled multi-station electronic universal testing machine has the characteristics of compact structure, simple and easy to use, can be applied to the maximum load of 10kN tensile, compression, bending, shearing, peeling, tearing and other force Testing.

The beam in the main engine is guided by the high precision guide bar, which has high rigidity and good linear motion characteristics. Load sensors with different measuring ranges can provide accurate measurement data for samples ranging from micrometer fibers to full-load structural parts. In addition, rich fixture tooling, deformation measurement and other accessories, almost can meet all customer needs.

The flexible and easy-to-use Testexpert software makes the testing process easier for users. Pilot solutions cover plastics, metals, automotive industry, building materials, textiles, paper and board, food and packaging, composite materials, etc.
