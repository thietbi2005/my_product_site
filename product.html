HST-GJW-E Series Computer Controlled Voltage Breakdown Tester

Mainly applied in solid insulating materials such as: plastics, films, resins, mica, ceramic, glass, insulation paint, and other media in the power frequency voltage or DC voltage breakdown strength and withstanding voltage Test of time; The instrument is controlled by computer, can during the test data fast and accurate collection, processing, and can access, display, print.
