Device for determining the strength of the sole and welt fastening (up to 3 kN)

Application

: The device is designed to determine the strength of the fastening of the sole and welt in welted shoes, soles and blanks in shoes for doppel and sandal fastening of leather shoes with nail, screw, wooden-studded, stitched fastening of soles at a temperature of (20 +15 -10 ) Â° C according to GOST 9134 -78. The maximum width of the fastened end is 40 mm, the maximum thickness of the fastened end is 28 mm. The fixture is attached to the standard grips of the Testing machine in the tensile zone using rectangular adapters. The device is allowed to be used with a load of up to 3 kN.
