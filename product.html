HST- MX01 Coefficient Of Friction Tester

The friction coefficient Tester is developed for the determination of static and dynamic friction coefficients of plastic films and sheets when sliding on the surface of themselves or other materials. It can meet GB/T1006-1988 "Plastics-Film and Sheet-Determination of Friction Coefficient".It is an ideal Test equipment for quality inspection departments and production enterprises at all levels, and can provide you with accurate test results.

1) Meet international standards, multiple Testing methods;

2) Can preset to meet one or some standards of GB, ISO, and ASTM;

3) Flexible driver system ensures stable operation and high accuracy;

4) Microcomputer control, LCD touch display shows the test value and curves;

5) Automatic analysis of friction state and quantitative discrete;

6) Kinetic friction test, static friction test and kinetic-static friction test are available;

7) Multiple report formats;

8) Can save test data of 6 different tests.
