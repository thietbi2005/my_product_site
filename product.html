Dichloromethane Test Machine

The Methylene Chloride Soak Tester  is used to make dichloromethane soak Test for plastic pipes, fittings, sheet material and so on .it is applied to production, test and research department of plastic pipes, fittings, plates. It accords with ISO/DIS 9852:2006.

Performance characteristics of Dichloromethane Test machine:

1. Automated control: easy to operate, press the start button after placing the sample, automatically drop to the position of methylene chloride erosion, and automatically time, automatically rise and alarm when finished.

2. Intelligent temperature control: The intelligent water circulation system is adopted, and the temperature control system controls the temperature accuracy by the intelligent instrument.
