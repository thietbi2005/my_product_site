PLC Control Automatic Quick Upset Testing Machine

Performance characteristics: DJ series PLC  upset Testing machines is designed according to the international standard "Metallic Materials Upset Test Methods", especially for the quality inspection of high-speed rods and wires. Through simulating the quick impact conditions of the metallic materials during the upset and pressing, the machine truthfully reflects the instances of the quickly compressed rods and wires. Under room temperature or heated-condition, exert test force in the direction of specimen axial line, quickly compress the specimen, and then inspect the metal ability of bearing upset deformation in the specified upset ratio. It also can show the disfigurements of metal surface. Applications: Metal rod, wire,; Fastener, standard parts, tools; Applied Standards: YQ-Z series is designed according to the International Standard "Metallic Materials Upset Test Methods". Software Graph: The software is built with multilanguage, English included.
