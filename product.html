HST-N200W 20-200Nm Desktop Metal Material Torsion Testing Machine

This machine is mainly used for the detection of torsional mechanical properties of metal materials and bolts, tools, finished products, structural parts, drive shafts, parts, fasteners, etc. at room temperature or under special circumstances.

Also it is used to Test the performance of the torsion destruction of non-metallic materials and finished products.

The test sample is installed between the clamps of the adjustable space to adapt to the test of the torsional mechanical properties of different tested samples.
