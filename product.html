GW-BT40H/50H Hydraulic Bending And Re-bending Testing Machine

Application

: Hydraulic Bending And Re-bending testing machine is a special equipment to carry on bending Test for reinforce bar, steel bar and pipe. It can do bending test with both directions according to standards requirement.

It conforms to standards ASTM A615-89, ASTM A615M-89, ISO 7438:1985, ISO8491:1986(E).
