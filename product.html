Electric push pull force test vertical machine

MainApplication

The integrated Testing machine is an integrated testing machine dedicated to Testing tension or compression. Replace traditional electric (manual) Test machine+The Test method of digital display table enhances the accuracy of test and the convenience of operation. It is suitable for the testing of push-pull load, insertion force, destruction test of rubber and plastic, light industrial textile, building doors and windows, composite materials, wire and cable, auto parts, power machinery, scientific research institutions and other industries.

With emergency stop function.
