Device for determination of puncture resistance (up to 3 kN)

Application

: The device is designed to determine the puncture resistance of special clothing or materials for its manufacture at a temperature of (20 +15 -10 ) Â° C according to GOST R 12.4198-99, GOST 12.4.241-2013. The diameter of the fixed sample is from 50 to 70 mm, the maximum thickness of the fixed sample is 5 mm. The fixture is attached to the standard adapters of the Testing machine in the compression zone. The device is allowed to be used with a load of up to 3 kN.
