Pneumatic Clamping Fixture Grips For Universal Testing Machine

Application

Range

* This pneumatic clamping fixture is suitable for tensile Test test of wire specimen. 

* Type of loading: Tension. Not suitable for through zero / reverse stress or fatigue Testing 

* Specimen material: Fine wires, cords, yarns, mono-filaments 

* Specimen shapes: Round

Features

* The clamp is pneumatically clamped. * Clamping method: After the upper clamp automatically clamps the specimen, the lower clamp automatically clamps the specimen after the predetermined load is given.

* Adjustable gripping force to reduce breakage at the clamping point

* Quick grip release for increased productivity 

*Optional automatic air kit with pretension and remote control for easy loading and improved efficiency  

Specifications

A specially designed horn with a smooth finish and a contoured surface with a graduated cam allows for easy loading and a stress reduced clamping area on the specimen. 

The clamping mechanism can be activated either automatically or through a foot switch.
